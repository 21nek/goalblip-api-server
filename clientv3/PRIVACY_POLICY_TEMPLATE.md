# GoalBlip Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: [Tarih]

## 1. Introduction

GoalBlip ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

GoalBlip stores the following information **only on your device** using local storage (AsyncStorage):

- **Favorite Matches**: Match IDs that you mark as favorites
- **Language Preference**: Your selected language (Turkish, English, Spanish)
- **Timezone Preference**: Your selected timezone
- **Time Format Preference**: Your preferred time format (12h/24h)

**Important**: This information is stored locally on your device and is **never transmitted to our servers or any third parties**.

### 2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **NOT** collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Any data that can identify you personally

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally on your device is used solely to:
- Remember your favorite matches
- Maintain your language and timezone preferences
- Provide a personalized experience within the app

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No data is shared with third parties
- You can delete all stored data by uninstalling the app

## 5. Third-Party Services

GoalBlip connects to our own API server to fetch match data and statistics. This connection:
- Does not transmit any personal information
- Only requests publicly available match data
- Uses standard HTTPS encryption (in production)

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete all stored data by uninstalling the app
- Clear app data through your device settings
- Stop using the app at any time

## 7. Children's Privacy

GoalBlip is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: [your-email@example.com]
- Website: [your-website.com]
